Megha Institute of Engineering and Technology for Women, Ranga Reddy was established in 2008 under the auspice of Crescent Educational Society. Eminent educationalists involved in the field of education, for over a decade, formed the governing body of the College. Vision of the Institute is To be the engineering college of choice in Telangana that can provide the best learning experience, most productive learning environment in engineering education

Mission of the Institute is To develop global technocrats with human values by making positive difference in the lives of the students to develop their highest potential by providing eminent & committed faculty who are competent and caring

Institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and affiliated to JNT University, Hyderabad
